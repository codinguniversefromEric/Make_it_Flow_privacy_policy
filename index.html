Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 11, 2026

Thank you for choosing Make it Flow. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

1. 100% On-Device Processing

Make it Flow is built with a privacy-first approach. All document processing, including PDF parsing, optical character recognition (OCR), layout analysis, and EPUB generation, happens strictly on your device. We do not upload, store, or transmit your documents, images, or extracted text to any external cloud servers.

2. Data Collection and Advertising

To support the free version of Make it Flow, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized ads.

  • Advertising Identifier (IDFA): With your explicit consent via the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt, we may allow AdMob to collect your device's advertising identifier to show you more relevant ads.
  • Analytics & Telemetry: Ad providers may collect non-personally identifiable information such as device type, OS version, and network status to ensure the proper functioning and measurement of ad delivery.

If you upgrade to our Premium version via an In-App Purchase, the Google AdMob SDK is entirely disabled, and no advertising-related data is collected.

3. In-App Purchases

We use Apple's native StoreKit to process In-App Purchases. We do not collect or store your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID. All transactions are securely processed and managed by Apple. Apple may share non-identifiable purchase statuses with us to unlock premium features.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
